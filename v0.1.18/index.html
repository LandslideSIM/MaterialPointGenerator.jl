MaterialPointGenerator.jlMPM model preprocessor

Efficiently generate structured (regular) particles.

View on GitHub 👀
🕸️

Structured Particle

The spacing of the generated particles in the X, Y, and Z directions is uniform (regular).

📏

Complicated 2/3D model

Support complex models and allow for partitioning of particles to facilitate the assignment of different attributes to particles in MPM simulations.

⛰️

Digital Elevation Model

A variety of useful functions have been developed for DEM scenarios, which can facilitate the generation of computing models for landslides.

💨

Efficiency

The processing speed of all methods is at the level of seconds, with no performance issues.
